Vocabulary Quiz

1. If you _____________ tacos to pizza, you like tacos better.

a. force
b. prefer
c. trace

2. A soccer field has a _________________ on each end.

a. goal
b. trace
c. patient

3. When many people have the same name, the name is _______________.

a. common
b. faint
c. firm

4. If your parents __________ you to stay up late, they give you permission to do so.

a. trace
b. force
c. allow

5. A _______________ person may feel angry because of a bad experience.

a. firm
b. bitter
c. common

6. To be ____________ is to be calm and understanding.

a. bitter
b. faint
c. patient

7. ______________ pencil marks are light and difficult to see.

a. faint
b. patient
c. firm

8. If you ___________________ open a box, you use your strength to get inside it.

a. force
b. faint
c. trace

9. About twenty-five people work at the ________________.

a. firm
b. goal
c. patient

10. When you _______________ a design, you draw over its lines on another sheet of paper.

a. allow
b. trace
c. prefer
